About PrimeWheels

PrimeWheels is a private hire service based in Umm Al Quwain, covering all Emirates — Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, RAK, Abu Dhabi and more.

The idea is simple: give customers the comfort of a private car with the honesty of a meter taxi. Clean cars, clear prices, and a driver who respects your time. No rush, no attitude — just smooth, relaxed trips.

Whether you need an early-morning airport drop, daily office rides, school pickups, family shopping runs or long-distance travel, PrimeWheels can handle it with the same car and the same friendly driver you trust.
